Shoes

    This is a graphic that I made for my Marketing 380 class. In this course, we were assigned to come up with a product and market it to the professor in a formal presentation. I created a graphic for the concept of shoes with soles made of recycled Styrofoam. I used a light background and simple font in order to direct emphasize towards the product. Created using: Adobe Photoshop.

Folk Art

    This piece is based off of Polish folk art. This art style typically features chickens and flowers mirrored symmetrically. Colors traditionally seen in Polish artwork are included in this picture, such as pastels and bright pinks and blues. Created using: Procreate

The Catcher in the Rye
